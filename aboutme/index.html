My name is Kim Sorensen. I have lived in American Fork, UT, for 31 years.
I was born in a hospital right across the street from the Idaho Falls Temple and,
21 years later, was sealed to my eternal companion, Phil, in the Idaho Falls Temple.
We have three children, two sons, and a daughter. We have two granddaughters and I
am blessed to spend time with them each week.

I enjoy hand spining, which means I own spinning wheels and use them to make yarn.
I also love to knit, weave, dye, garden, and do family history research.

I attended a boot camp last year and learned that I enjoy creating websites.
I am excited to finish the education that I began years ago at Ricks College.
Online education works great for me so that I can still work as a customer service representative,
volunteer, care for my family, and have time to finish my degree. My major is Applied Technology
and right now my goal is to work as a front end dev.
